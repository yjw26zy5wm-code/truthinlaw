About TruthInLaw

TruthInLaw is an independent educational initiative dedicated to helping people navigate legal information responsibly in the age of artificial intelligence.

As AI becomes a common source of legal information, many people unknowingly rely on answers that may contain fabricated court cases, incorrect statutes, or outdated legal guidance. TruthInLaw exists to educate the public, promote critical thinking, and encourage verification before legal decisions are made.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve public understanding of AI-generated legal information while providing free educational resources that help people distinguish trustworthy legal information from misinformation.

Our Principles

Accuracy

We believe important legal information should always be verified before it is trusted.

Accessibility

Everyone deserves access to reliable legal education regardless of background or income.

Responsibility

Artificial intelligence should assist human judgment—not replace it.

Looking Forward

TruthInLaw began as a public educational resource. As the platform grows, we aim to build practical tools that help people verify AI-generated legal information, improve digital literacy, and make informed decisions with greater confidence.
